Resistance to cracking

G*.Sin δ, as determined by a dynamic shear rheometer (DSR), is used to control fatigue cracking in the SUPERPAVE specifications, and is based in part on the results of controlled-strain fatigue tests that were developed as a part of the SHRP research effort (Deacon et al, 1997). The lower the value of G*.Sin δ, the higher the resistance of the binder to fatigue cracking.

Binder viscous component

The phase angle gives an indication of the ratio of the viscous component to the elastic component. As the phase angle increases, so the viscous component increases, resulting in improved resistance to cracking for the same value of G*.

Resistance against abrasion

Abrasion loss is affected by means of the Los Angeles Abrasion machine. The percent of weight loss (Cantabro loss) is an indication of durability and relates to an improvement in the quality of the asphalt binder, which is indicated by improved cohesion. This test method determines the abrasion loss of 150 mm cores which have been trimmed to a height of 10 mm

About Us

The function of HER is quite complex. See Agre’ment certification and the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR) evaluation of the product. HER can be seen to give many benefits to ageing asphalt and has an accumulative effect, based on a repetitive application of every 5 to 6 years for roads and 4 to 5 years for runways. The variation in frequency is due to variables in traffic loading, environmental exposure and the condition of the existing bituminous surface. Some of our clients chose to apply at a frequency of 3 years based on their own assessment of their estate roads purely on maintaining property values within the estate.
1. Re-establishes the flexibility of the existing bituminous surface to prevent brittle oxidization cracking.
2. Seals micro cracks in the existing bituminous surface and improves impermiability protecting the substrata.
3. Maintains friction levels, ie does not become slippery when wet.
4. Aesthetically pleasing in appearance, Maintains the visual appearance of a well-maintained estate.
5. The manually applied product not only produces job opportunities but also allows for access control to individual driveways. Allowing infilling of omitted sections when treated road is dry.

Overview

HER Rejuvenation is a sophisticated blend of high-quality bitumen-friendly solvents, oils, peptizers, and naturally occurring bitumen (Hydrocarbons). This product is not merely a manufacturer’s claim but an internationally recognized assessment by the Agrément board, esteemed globally.

Agrément assessment
Certification and Evaluation

The HER Rejuvenator's certification required rigorous evaluation by the Council for Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR). The product restores the bitumen properties lost due to oxidation. The rejuvenator penetrates the surface, replenishing bitumen properties, while the residual bitumen forms a barrier that oxidizes slowly. This penetration also seals micro-cracks on the surface without compromising road or runway friction. The critical properties for HER Rejuvenator are detailed below. (Certificate Number 2016/516)

Agrément assessment
Performance and Standards

HER Rejuvenator has been tested on 50/70 penetration grade bitumen; the hardest bitumen typically used on road surfaces. It is manufactured uniformly worldwide, adhering to strict industry standards. The CSIR regulates its performance through audited manufacturing processes. Each batch undergoes rigorous testing, and samples are retained for future research. Meeting these measurable properties ensures the product's effectiveness as determined by the CSIR. Improvements in impermeability and friction can validated on-site using the British Pendulum Test and the “Marvil” Permeability Test before work commencement.
